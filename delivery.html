Delivery Policy

Pedro Honeker Gallinger · artist@pedrohonekergallinger.art



Packaging

Original canvases are shipped unstretched, rolled inside a rigid cardboard or PVC tube, with protective layers to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. This method allows for international shipping at reduced cost and eliminates the risk of frame damage during transit.

Shipping Time

Processing time after payment confirmation is typically 5–10 business days. International shipping via courier (DHL, FedEx, or equivalent) generally takes 7–21 business days depending on the destination country and customs procedures.

Delivery times may vary due to customs procedures in the destination country. The customer is responsible for any applicable import duties or taxes.

Tracking

Once your artwork has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number by email so you can follow your shipment in real time.

Delivery Address

It is essential that the registered delivery address is correct and complete, without discrepancies or typographical errors. If the customer provides an incorrect address, the artwork will be returned to us automatically. Reshipping costs in this case will be borne by the customer.

Failed Delivery

In locations where home delivery is restricted, or after two delivery attempts where the recipient is absent, the artwork will be available at the nearest carrier office for seven calendar days. If not collected, it will be returned to us and reshipping costs will apply.

International Shipping for Institutional Projects

For large-scale works, murals, and institutional commissions, shipping logistics are coordinated individually through our Private Art Concierge service. Please contact us at artist@pedrohonekergallinger.art to discuss the specific requirements of your project.

Insurance

All international shipments are insured for their full declared value. In the unlikely event that an artwork arrives damaged due to transit conditions, the customer must report the issue within 48 hours of receipt, accompanied by photographs of the package and the artwork.

Contact

For any questions regarding shipping, tracking, or delivery, please contact us at artist@pedrohonekergallinger.art.

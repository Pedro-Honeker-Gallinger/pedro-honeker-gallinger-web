In Case of Exchange

  • Send an email to artist@pedrohonekergallinger.art stating the reason and attaching photos of the artwork and its packaging.
  • Exchanges are not accepted without the original packaging.
  • We are not responsible for artworks returned poorly packaged that arrive with transport damage.
  • The customer is responsible for the transportation costs of the returned piece.

In Case of Return

  • Send an email to artist@pedrohonekergallinger.art stating the reason and attaching photos of the artwork.
  • We will send you a shipping label so you can send it by courier or postal mail.
  • We do not accept returns without the original packaging.
  • The refund will be made when the artwork arrives at our address undamaged. If the piece arrives damaged despite proper packaging, we will analyze whether it was the carrier's fault and proceed accordingly.
  • The refund timeframe depends on the payment method used.


Important Observations

  • It is essential that the registered delivery address is correct, without typographical errors.
  • In locations where home delivery is restricted, or after two failed delivery attempts, the artwork will be available at the nearest carrier office for seven calendar days.
  • If the artwork is not collected within this period, it will be returned to us and the customer will be responsible for any reshipping costs.
  • For any doubts before purchase, please contact us — we are always happy to advise you.

Contact

For any questions regarding returns or exchanges, please write to artist@pedrohonekergallinger.art.

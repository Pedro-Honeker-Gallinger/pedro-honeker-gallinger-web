Returns & Exchanges

Pedro Honeker Gallinger · [email protected]

The consumer has every right to exchange or return original artworks within the period stipulated in our exchange policy. However, I kindly ask you to measure your space carefully before placing an order, as all artworks are original, unique pieces — not mass-produced reproductions.

If you have any doubts about sizing or which work suits your space best, I am available on WhatsApp to help you personally, free of charge.

For a return or exchange, the artwork must be in its original packaging with all transport protections and must be requested by email within 7 calendar days of receiving the piece.

In Case of Exchange

  • Send an email to [email protected] stating the reason and attaching photos of the artwork and its packaging.
  • Exchanges are not accepted without the original packaging.
  • We are not responsible for artworks returned poorly packaged that arrive with transport damage.
  • The customer is responsible for the transportation costs of the returned piece.

In Case of Return

  • Send an email to [email protected] stating the reason and attaching photos of the artwork.
  • We will send you a shipping label so you can send it by courier or postal mail.
  • We do not accept returns without the original packaging.
  • The refund will be made when the artwork arrives at our address undamaged. If the piece arrives damaged despite proper packaging, we will analyze whether it was the carrier's fault and proceed accordingly.
  • The refund timeframe depends on the payment method used.
PLEASE NOTE: We do not exchange or refund commissioned (custom) artworks. For a commissioned piece, I hold a virtual meeting with the client to discuss ideas. When finished, I show the result via photographs before shipping. Once approved by the client, the commission is considered final.
